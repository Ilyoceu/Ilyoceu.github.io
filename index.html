Dave Eric Grohl werd geboren op 14 januari 1969

in 1986, Dave Grohl joined Scream After Scream broke up in 1990, Grohl became the drummer of Nirvana, After the suicide of Kurt Cobain in 1994 Nirvana disbanded and Dave Grohl formed Foo Fighters, Grohl is also the drummer and co-founder of the Them Crooked Vultures and has recorded / toured with Queens of the Stone Age and Tenacious D. Dave Grohl was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame as a member of Nirvana in 2014 and as a member of the Foo Fighters in 2021

In 2021 Grohl released an autobiography: The storyteller: Tales of Life and Music
